TEKZIPARK

By TEKZITEL PTY LTD

TEKZIPARK is a battery powered smart parking sensor to provide occupancy status updates of parking spots wirelessly to your cloud environment. TEKZIPARK uses radar technology to detect vehicle presence thereby providing a high degree of certainty of vehicle presence/absence.
