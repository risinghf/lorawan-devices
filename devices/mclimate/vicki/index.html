MClimate Vicki - Smart Radiator Thermostat

By MClimate

MClimate Vicki is a a smart thermostatic radiator valve (TRV). It controls residential and commercial hot water radiators, measures temperature and humidity and the user can adjust the target temperature by the LEDs screen and rotating the exterior of the device.
